As is exhibited in the bar chart, the gains of participating in labor practice classes shows great diversity. To be specific, the percentage of improving labor capabilities, acquiring relevant knowledge, enhancing ability of collaboration and feeling comfortable accounts for 84.8%, 91.3%, 32.6%,54.4% respectively. Especially, acquiring relevant knowledge ranks the first.

Several factors contribute to the tendency. To begin with, a rising number of individuals are coming to the realization that labor practice can teach them knowledge unattainable through conventional instruction, which may boost their competence in the near future employment market. Moreover, it has been widely accepted that labor capability plays a crucial role in every aspects of life. Lastly, labor practice has a beneficial influence on personal growth, which will turn into the power that pushes the society to move forward, contributing to a faster social progress.

In general, it is not difficult to draw the conclusion that the labor practice classes should gain more attention by universities. With sustained efforts in practice education improvement , students practice abilities will also witness a bigger and faster growth in the years to come.
